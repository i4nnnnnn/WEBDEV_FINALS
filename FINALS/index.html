Our Story

About Us

The Legacy

Healing Since 2024

What started as a modest community movement has evolved into a cornerstone of animal welfare. At PAWNDERS, we believe that the best rescue work is a mix of high-end digital precision and the human touch. We don't just save lives; we care for souls.

The Beginning

Founded by visionary advocates who wanted to make animal welfare accessible to every family in Valenzuela.

Innovation Era

Implementing digital tracking for rescues and AI-powered health monitoring for stray rehabilitation.

Present Day

Serving over 5,000 rescues annually with a 98% adoption success rate, prioritizing safety and transparency.
