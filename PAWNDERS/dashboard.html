About Us

The Mission

Compassion Since 2026

What started as a student initiative at OLFU has evolved into a vital digital reporting platform dedicated to animal welfare. At StraySafe PH, we believe community-driven reporting and swift action can solve the stray crisis one paw at a time. By bridging the gap between local residents and veterinary responders, we empower the citizens of Valenzuela City to act as the eyes and ears for those who cannot speak for themselves.

The Foundation

Founded by student developers to bridge the gap between spotting strays and providing medical aid.

Tech For Good

In 2026, we scaled to a real-time dashboard integrating veterinary clinics and volunteer responders.

Impact Driven

Empowering residents of Valenzuela City to act as the eyes and ears for those who cannot speak for themselves.
