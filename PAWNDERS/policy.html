Privacy Policy

At StraySafe PH, we prioritize the protection of your personal information. This policy explains how we handle your data within the Valenzuela City stray response ecosystem.

  • Information Collection: We collect names, contact details, and location data specifically when you report an incident via Pawmalasakit.
  • Data Usage: Your data is used exclusively to coordinate animal rescues and verify the authenticity of reports.
  • Safety: We do not sell or trade your data to third-party advertisers.

Terms of Service

By using the StraySafe PH platform, you agree to follow our community guidelines aimed at ensuring the safety of both humans and animals.

  • Reporting Integrity: You agree to provide accurate information. False reports delay emergency responses for animals in actual need.
  • Code of Conduct: Users must interact with the Pawmana Hub and community dashboard with respect. Harassment or abuse of the system will lead to account termination.
  • Liability: StraySafe PH acts as a digital response manager. While we facilitate interventions, we are not responsible for the physical actions of individual volunteers.

Cookie Policy

To make your "Member Portal" experience smoother, we use cookies to remember your login state and preferences.

  • Essential Cookies: These are required to keep you signed in to your dashboard.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand which sections (like Furcast or Pawmana) are most utilized by the community.
  • Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that some glassmorphism UI elements and login features may not function as intended.
